Why strong passwords matter

Weak passwords are vulnerable to brute-force and dictionary attacks, where attackers rapidly test huge numbers of likely combinations. Longer passwords with mixed character types dramatically increase the total search space and the time needed to crack them.

For best protection, use unique passwords for each account and enable multi-factor authentication (MFA).
